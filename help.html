HOW YOU CAN HELP

We are fighting to bring healing to our environment by stablizing the world's most endangered species.

Down below are ways you can be involved

Buy elephant-friendly coffee and wood

Coffee and timber crops are often grown in plantations that destroy elephant habitats.

Donate

Just by donating just a little bit of money can help save and build environments for endangered animals.

Eat for the planet

Just by donating just a little bit of money can help save and build environments for endangered animals.

Take shorter showers

Showering for one minute less than usual could save between 9-15 litres of water. Using products like leave in conditioner can help you shave off some time. This in turn leaves more water for the animals.

Fund the endangered speices act

When a species becomes endangered, it is a sign that the ecosystem is slowly falling apart. Funding this cause will help endangered species recieve protection.

Adopt an animal

Adopting an animal can put them in a safe environment and in this way they can be protected.
